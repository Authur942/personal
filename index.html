Lamborghini

development history

Engineer gian Paolo Dallara has improved the V12 engine of 350gt to a maximum power of 320 HP, which is used in 400gt. At the 1966 Geneva Motor Show, Lamborghini officially released the 400gt. After the launch of 400gt, the car was recognized by consumers. Lamborghini then launched a variety of versions of the 400gt model. In 1968, Lamborghini launched two models, Islero GT and Miura P400. The former was designed by the former Maserati designer, which made the Islero GT have the shadow of many Maserati models, which was criticized by many consumers. Another car, Miura P400, has attracted wide attention. This car is the first sports car with a mid engine layout in Lamborghini, creating a precedent in the design of the mid engine in Lamborghini.

development history

Ferugio Lamborghini's Tractor Company began to experience a financial crisis. Cento tratori's, the largest customer of Lamborghini tractors, the South African importer, cancelled the long-term contract with Lamborghini, which caused a great loss to the sales of Lamborghini. In 1972, ferugio Lamborghini sold the Lamborghini Tractor Company to the Italian agricultural equipment manufacturer, same, and focused on the operation of Lamborghini automobile company. However, Lamborghini automobile company began to lose financial support gradually, and the speed of model development also slowed down gradually.
